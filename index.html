CRAFTING STYLE WITH SURGICAL PRECISION

Prince is a highly professional, licensed barber with over a decade of experience in crafting clean styles, complex geometric patterns, and classic razor details. Renowned for his surgical precision and patient demeanor, Prince has made Urban Hairstylin the premier grooming destination in Tema. Location & Driving Directions

FIND US IN NEW SEBREPOR

We are located in New Sebrepor, near the Coastal Estate, Tema, Ghana. Our location is easily accessible from the main roads and has plenty of free secure street parking.

From Coastal Estate

Head north from the Coastal Estate exit onto the main New Sebrepor connection road. Look for our sign on your right hand side, next to the modern commercial plaza.

From Michel Camp Junction

Drive towards the Coastal Estate axis; we are situated in the heart of the developing Sebrepor community. Prince's shop stands as a clean, luxurious black-and-gold storefront.
